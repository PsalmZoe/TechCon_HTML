Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has since grown into one of the most influential technology conferences worldwide. From humble beginnings in a local auditorium to international venues hosting thousands of attendees, TechCon continues to inspire and innovate.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster global innovation, empower emerging talent, and create a collaborative space for sharing revolutionary ideas in technology. We are committed to accessibility, diversity, and sustainability in all aspects of the conference.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Maya Chen

Renowned AI researcher and professor, Dr. Maya Chen shared groundbreaking insights on ethical AI development and was instrumental in forming collaborative research programs post-conference.

Elijah Brooks

Entrepreneur and open-source advocate, Elijah delivered a compelling keynote on decentralized systems and their role in democratizing technology across the globe.

Amina Rahman

A leader in sustainable hardware development, Amina's workshops on green tech have led to new initiatives in several global tech firms following her appearance at TechCon.
